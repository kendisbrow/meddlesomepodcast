Upskilling, incentivizing, blue skying, and core competencies. Pretty obtuse.  Terms of Art or purposeful obfuscation? Here are some stories about the butchering of language, why it's done, and how it interferes with real communication. Next time you're in a meeting and hear five of these phrase shout out "Bingo!" In this episode, Jim and Ken investigate the pervasive use of jargon in the consulting industry and how it obscures effective communication.
